What the terms mean
Total Equity: the combined account value across all five venues.
P&L Today / 7d: realized profit or loss over those windows (closed trades).
Open P/L: the unrealized profit/loss on positions still held right now.
P/L $ / %: per-position unrealized gain/loss since entry (live, marked to last price).
Futures Gate (x/25): the futures strategy must complete 25 paper trades and prove itself before it is allowed to trade real money.
