Moonshot — Convexity Research

A research sleeve asking one question: can we spot, ahead of time, a stock about to move far more than the options market expects (an explosive / "black-swan" move)? It is research only — not trading real money. Every signal must beat the market's own implied probability on out-of-sample data before it could ever be funded.

What the terms mean
Verdict: the research system's honest call — does the convexity signal have a real, tradeable edge, or is the market priced fairly?
Bronze / Silver / Gold: the data pipeline stages — raw captured data → cleaned & labelled features → the ranked shortlist of explosive candidates.
P(explosive): model-estimated probability a ticker makes an outsized move before its options expire.
ATM IV: at-the-money implied volatility — how big a move the options market is pricing in.
IV rank: where today's IV sits versus its own history (0 = cheapest in range, 1 = most expensive).
GEX: gamma exposure — dealer option positioning that can dampen (positive) or amplify (negative) price moves.
